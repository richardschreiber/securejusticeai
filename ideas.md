## Selected Approach: Neo-Brutalist Institutional

**Rationale**: This approach best captures the gravity of the "Justice Gap" while projecting the stability and authority required for a legal aid solution. The "Box" motif aligns perfectly with the product name, and the high-contrast style ensures accessibility and readability, which are critical for this audience. It avoids the generic "SaaS startup" look (often associated with the Modern Humanist style) and instead feels like a serious, established infrastructure project.

**Design Philosophy Statement**:
"We are building a digital infrastructure for justice. Our design must reflect stability, urgency, and absolute clarity. We reject decorative fluff in favor of bold information architecture that highlights the crisis (the problem) and the structured solution (the box)."
